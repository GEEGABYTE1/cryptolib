The World is Changing with Upcoming Technology

The world is changing rapidly, and one the biggest factors that is shifting humanity to the next generation of technology is blockchain. You've probably heard of cryptocurrencies, NFTS, or the metaverse... All of those are created through blockchain networks.
Blockchain has a wide range of applications not just in cryptocurrencies and in the Web3 world, but in the political world as well. Soverignty, legitimacy, and most importantly, human rights are starting to be impacted due to the presence of blockchain technology.
It is important that we understand how the next generation of technology will change our lives, and currently, there is a lot of information coming out about the blockchain industry.
